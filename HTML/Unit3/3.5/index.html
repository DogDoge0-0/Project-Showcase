How To Play & Keybinds

  1. Drag your mouse across the screen to position the paddle.

  2. Use the paddle to bounce the ball towards the bricks.

  3. Destory all of the bricks to win the level
  • Press esc or space to pause/play the game.
  • Press the arrow keys to move the paddle.

Please Note:

Issues with the ball speed and flickering bricks are due to the browser that you are using, for optimal gaming, play on firefox or edge.
