2.4: Responsive Portfolio


This page was the first one I made with Bootstrap, a toolkit originally developed by employees at Twitter that is free for anyone in the world to use. Bootstrap is the reason you can view this site properly on mobile devices (I always design my sites for computers first and phones second). The site features actual images that I took on a plane not too long ago. The contact page on this site (as you can tell) is fake, but the images next to the types of contacts are SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) icons, not actual images. This page also introduced me to the !important rule in CSS, which prioritizes properties that have the rule attached. This is very important when developing a site using a toolkit.
