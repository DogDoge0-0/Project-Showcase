Homemade Pizza

This is my own creation, with help from my dad. This pizza is a great way to spend some time with friends or family, as the recipe is built to let as many people as you want, make their own.

Pieces of the Pie

  • Premade Dough
  • Ragu Homemade Style Pizza Sauce
  • Sargento Six Cheese Italian Fine Cut Cheese
  • BelGiorgio Mozzarella Cheese
  • Any Toppings
  • Baking Pan large enough to hold your dough
  • Aluminum Foil

Assembling the Pie

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F or 175°C.
  2. Spread Ragu Homemade Style Pizza Sauce on the dough evenly.
  3. Sprinkle some Sargento Six Cheese Italian Fine Cut Cheese onto the pizza.
  4. Add some BelGiorgio Mozzarella Cheese to the pizza, with at least 0.5in (1.3cm) spacing between the balls.
  5. Add any toppings you enjoy on a pizza
  6. Cover your baking pan with Aluminum Foil.
  7. Put your pie onto the aluminum foil covered baking pan.
  8. Put your pan and pie into the middlemost rack of your oven.
  9. Cook for 12 to 14 minutes or until the center is hot.
  10. Remove your pie from the oven and let it cool for one minute.
