How Birch Wathen Leonx was Founded

(A not true story)

Once upon a time...

There were three friends, Birch, Wathen and Lenox. They met in high school. Birch was an outgoing 'cool' kid. Wathen exelled in organization, he had the best handwriting. Lenox was the best at writing, he helped everyone with their essays.

After becoming best friends, and graduating university, they split up to persue their own seperate interests.

One day they all met in a park and decided to stat a new orginization. They had all made fortunes in their practaces and wanted to give back to the comunity.

Their idea was a school, to teach the youngsters everything they need to know to make fortunes like the trio.

Over 100 years later, their legacy remains.
